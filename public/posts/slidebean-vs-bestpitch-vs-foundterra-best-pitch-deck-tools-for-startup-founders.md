## Frequently Asked Questions
### Do I need a professional designer for my pitch deck?
Not necessarily. Using high-quality templates or AI tools can achieve a professional look, provided your narrative and financial data are strong.

### How much should I pay for a pitch deck?
Costs vary wildly. Templates range from $50–$200, while comprehensive advisory services like Foundterra offer fixed-price packages that save you the time and risk of "do-it-yourself" fundraising.

### Does a good design guarantee funding?
No. Design builds trust, but investors fund based on market size, team capability, and clear financial projections. Your deck is a tool, not the final decision maker.

### Should I use success-fee based consultants?
Be careful. High-quality advisors usually charge fixed fees; success-fee models can create conflicts of interest and often signal a lack of confidence in the startup's own merit.
